CHALLENGE

For Pasta House one of the main challenges was capturing the essence of a brand rooted in friendship and a shared passion for pasta, while maintaining a refined yet approachable visual identity. The business, started by two friends, needed to feel personal and warm without veering into overly casual or kitschy territory. Striking the right balance between sophistication and playfulness was key, especially in a crowded market where many pasta brands lean heavily into either traditional or overly trendy aesthetics.

SOLUTION

The solution came in form of a logo that echoes the elasticity of pasta dough, fluid, stretched, and organic in form. The use of a primarily black-and-white palette with the addition of muted tones and a strategic pop of yellow introduced just enough vibrancy to feel lively without being loud. This minimal but expressive approach allowed the branding to stay versatile across mediums while reinforcing the handcrafted feel of the product and the easy-going, authentic dynamic between the two founders.
