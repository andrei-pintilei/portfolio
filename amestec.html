CHALLENGE

For Amestec Festival, the challenge was to visually express the energy and diversity suggested by the name Amestec (meaning "blend"). The identity needed to feel lively and eclectic, while still being cohesive enough to work across various applications. It also had to capture the spirit of the festival’s natural setting.

SOLUTION

The solution was a playful, slightly chaotic wordmark using a 70s-inspired font that brings a retro, free-spirited vibe. Vibrant colors add to the sense of energy and variety, while minimal mountain illustrations subtly ground the identity in the festival’s scenic location. The result is a bold, fun visual system that reflects the mix of music, people, and landscape at the heart of Amestec.
