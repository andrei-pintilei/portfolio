CHALLENGE

Atelier 2BA needed a brand that communicated both clarity and creativity, something that felt modern, professional, and conceptually strong. The name itself, while unique, required a visual treatment that would unify the characters and subtly hint at the firm’s work without being too literal.

SOLUTION

The final logo is a minimalist monogram where the "2" "B" and "A" are combined into a single form. Two strong lines suggest the "2" while the overall shape also reveals the "B" and "A", forming a cohesive symbol that subtly echoes the outline of a roof. This approach created a mark that’s clean, versatile, and aligned with the firm’s aesthetic: understated but intentional, with a strong structural presence.
