CHALLENGE

The main challenge was evolving the identity from something soft but generic into a more distinctive and elevated expression that matched the brand’s high-quality knitwear. The original look didn’t fully communicate the sophistication of the products or set the brand apart in a competitive market. The goal was to retain the core idea of blooming, but reinterpret it with more intention and refinement.

SOLUTION

The new icon is a minimal, abstract bloom, designed to feel both elegant and modern. It became the focal point of the updated packaging, complemented by delicate hand-drawn floral illustrations for a subtle contrast. A combination of serif and sans serif fonts was used across materials to balance softness with structure, adding to the premium feel.
