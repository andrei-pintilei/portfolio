CHALLENGE

For the Jitar branding, the challenge was to create an identity that honored both the personal journey of the founders and the deep cultural roots of the region. After spending years working with winemakers in Italy, the owners returned home to start their own winery, bringing with them a mix of tradition, experience, and a strong sense of family. It was important that the branding reflected this story in a way that felt authentic and grounded.

SOLUTION

The logo draws inspiration from the ancient Cucuteni culture, which once thrived in the area. By combining symbolic elements found in Cucuteni artifacts, representing both femininity and masculinity, the mark subtly ties back to the family’s heritage and the balance at the heart of their story. The addition of the tagline "family winery" helps anchor the brand in this narrative, adding warmth and context while reinforcing the personal touch behind Jitar.
